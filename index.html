E6 - Mindstone

Mindstone is a device that transfers your thoughts directly from one user to the intented users' mindstone. The thoughts transferred here are primarily text based messages which also convey the underlying emotions or neurotramitters with which they were generated. Basic idea is what if you could Airdrop your thoughts to your friend at an instant. It is an inclusive innovation in the sense that any differently abled person with blindness, deafness or muteness can convey their thoughts easily without having a language barrier or skill issue for communicating with others. This device is perfect for on the go communication as well as has a privacy setting which gives you the power to decide whether to send the thought to whom and with or without the underlying emotion.
